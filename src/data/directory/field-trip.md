## Why It Matters

Field bloat is one of the most common issues in mature Salesforce orgs. Hundreds of custom fields that nobody uses, duplicates created by different teams, fields that were "temporary" three years ago. Field Trip gives you actual usage data — not guesses — so you can make informed decisions about what to keep, merge, or deprecate.

For architects doing org health assessments or planning migrations, field usage data is foundational. You can't simplify what you don't measure.

## Alternatives

- **Happy Soup** — broader dependency analysis, not field-specific
- **Salesforce Optimizer** — native tool, less granular
