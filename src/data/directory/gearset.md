## Why It Matters

Gearset has become the default recommendation for teams that want powerful DevOps without building their own pipeline from scratch. The metadata comparison engine is genuinely best-in-class — it catches deployment conflicts that Salesforce CLI misses. Intelligent merge conflict resolution, automated test scheduling, and org-to-org backup round out the package.

For architects, Gearset is the right call when the team needs speed-to-value on DevOps. It's not cheap, but it eliminates the overhead of maintaining custom scripts and pipeline YAML. The trade-off is vendor lock-in — your deployment logic lives in Gearset, not in your repo.

## Alternatives

- **Copado** — more ALM features, stronger in regulated industries
- **sfp (Flxbl)** — open-source, full control, steeper learning curve
- **DevOps Center** — free, Salesforce-native, but limited for complex orgs
