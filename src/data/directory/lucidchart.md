## Why It Matters

Every architect needs a diagramming tool, and Lucidchart is the industry standard for Salesforce solution design. ERDs, data flow diagrams, integration architecture, org strategy visuals — it handles all of them with real-time collaboration.

The Salesforce-specific shape library is a bonus — pre-built icons for clouds, objects, and platform components. For CTA candidates, Lucidchart is practically a requirement for mock scenario presentations.

## Alternatives

- **draw.io (diagrams.net)** — free, open-source alternative
- **Miro** — better for whiteboarding and workshops
- **Canva** — simpler, better for polished presentation visuals
