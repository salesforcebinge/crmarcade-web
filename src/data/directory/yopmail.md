## Why It Matters

Every Salesforce implementation needs test data, and test data needs email addresses. Yopmail gives you instant, disposable inboxes — no signup, no passwords. Create a Contact with `test-user@yopmail.com`, then check the inbox at yopmail.com to verify email deliverability, workflow alerts, or approval notifications.

It's a small tool, but it saves architects and testers hours of managing dummy email accounts during UAT and sandbox testing.

## Alternatives

- **Mailinator** — similar disposable email service
- **Guerrilla Mail** — temporary email with more privacy features
- **Salesforce Email Logs** — for verifying delivery without checking inboxes
