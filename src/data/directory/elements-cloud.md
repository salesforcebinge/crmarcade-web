## Why It Matters

Elements.cloud is one of those tools that becomes essential the moment you inherit a complex org. It connects to your Salesforce metadata and builds a visual map of everything — objects, fields, flows, process builders, validation rules — and how they relate to each other. Think Happy Soup's dependency analysis but with a full documentation and change management layer on top.

For architects doing org assessments, merger/acquisition consolidations, or technical debt cleanup, Elements.cloud turns weeks of manual discovery into hours. The business process documentation feature also bridges the gap between technical metadata and business context — something architects constantly struggle to maintain.

## Alternatives

- **Happy Soup** — free, focused on dependency analysis
- **Org Check** — free Salesforce Labs tool (replaced Salesforce Optimizer)
- **Metazoa Snapshot** — metadata comparison and documentation
