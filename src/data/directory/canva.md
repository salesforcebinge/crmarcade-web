## Why It Matters

Canva isn't a traditional architect tool, but it's becoming one. When you need to present a solution design to stakeholders who don't speak ERDs, Canva turns your architecture into something visually compelling. Clean diagrams, branded presentations, infographic-style solution overviews.

For architects who present to business stakeholders as much as technical teams, Canva bridges the gap between technical accuracy and executive readability.

## Alternatives

- **Lucidchart** — more powerful for technical diagrams
- **draw.io (diagrams.net)** — free, more diagram-focused
- **Google Slides / PowerPoint** — traditional presentation tools
