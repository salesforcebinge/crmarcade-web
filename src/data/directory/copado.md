## Why It Matters

Copado is the enterprise heavyweight in Salesforce DevOps. Where Gearset excels at deployment mechanics, Copado covers the full ALM lifecycle — user stories, sprint management, environment provisioning, quality gates, compliance controls, and release orchestration.

For architects designing delivery pipelines in regulated industries (financial services, healthcare, government), Copado's audit trail and compliance features often tip the decision. It also handles multi-cloud deployments (Salesforce + OmniStudio/Salesforce Industries). The trade-off is complexity and cost — it's a platform, not a tool, and requires dedicated admin attention.

## Alternatives

- **Gearset** — simpler, faster to adopt, stronger metadata comparison
- **sfp (Flxbl)** — open-source, maximum flexibility, no licensing cost
- **DevOps Center** — free, native, but not enterprise-ready
